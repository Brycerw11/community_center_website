Amenities:
This gym offers large speakers and controllable spotlights for making your event much more engaging. There are bathrooms behind the bleachers and plenty of trash cans. There's plenty of seating, concession stands already set up (you will need to bring your own food and drinks to put in the stands, though), and seating sections specially preserved for handicapped people.
